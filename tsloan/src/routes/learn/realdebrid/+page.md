This is a brief tutorial on setting up a Real Debrid Account.
1. Navigate to <a href="http://real-debrid.com/?id=11767646">Real-Debrid</a>
2. Sign up
3. Ensure you are logged in with the account you just created
4. Go to the section "Premium Offers" and choose the plan that works for you
    - While the purchases only allow you access for a length of time, they do not auto-renew. You may need to return to this step in the future
    - I recommend the longest plan option. It's the best deal
5. Follow the steps to purchase
6. Once you've purchased a plan, you should see a date under your account section showing the number of premium days you have left, as well as the day your plan will expire.
7. That's it! Short and sweet, you now have a RealDebrid account.
